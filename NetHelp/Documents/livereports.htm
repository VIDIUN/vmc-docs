Live Reports

The Live Reports tab is enabled in the Analytics section for each VMC account that has Live enabled.

Live Reports display how many plays were counted for live entries. The Live Report's display is similar to all the other Vidiun analytics reports.

Plays (for all type of live entries - Vidiun, Akamai, Manual) that are played through the Vidiun player  are counted and displayed at a 1 hour granularity (for example, number of plays at 2pm, number of plays at 3pm, etc.), and are updated every few hours as all of Vidiun analytics are.

The total number of plays is also displayed in the ‘Content > Entries’ tab, and is updated every 24 hours.
