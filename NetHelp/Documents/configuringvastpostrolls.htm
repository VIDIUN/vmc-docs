Configuring VAST Post Rolls

PostRoll ads are ads that appear after an online video.

To configure PostRoll ads

1.  Configure the Player Advertising Settings.

2.  Click on PostRoll Ads.

3.  Toggle on Request PostRoll Ads Enabled.

4.  Paste the Ad Tag URL received from the ad server. See How to Locate the Ad Tag URL.

5.  Select the Display options.
