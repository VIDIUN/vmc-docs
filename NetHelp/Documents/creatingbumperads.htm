Creating Bumper Ads

Bumpers are videos that act as ads and do not use an ad server. Bumper videos uploaded to Vidiun can be inserted before or after a video, to function as pre-rolls or post-rolls. Bumper videos are associated with a player, and are not associated with a specific video. Bumper videos are independent of actual pre/post-rolls and can be played in addition to ads. Bumper videos are helpful for Vidiun partners that would like to advertise their logo, or other information, before or after a video, and for smaller partners that would like to advertise, but do not expect to monetize from their ads.

Bumper videos can be configured in addition to any other ads that are configured. On the bottom of the Advertising tab, the option to add bumper videos only is available.

To configure a Bumper ads

1.  Go to the Studio tab and select Flash Studio.

2.  Select or create a player.

3.  Select the Advertising tab.

4.  In the Ad Timeline section, click on Bumper Video.

5.  Toggle on Bumper Video Enabled.

6.  Paste the Vidiun Entry ID that will be used as the bumper.

7.  Enter the URL in the Click URL field.

A Vidiun partner also has the ability to enable ONLY bumper ads by choosing Bumper Only as the Ad Source:

While in the Advertising tab, you can also configure bumper pre-rolls, and or bumper post-rolls.

To configure bumper post-rolls

1.  Create a bumper video. See Creating Bumper Ads.

2.  In the Features tab on the left, select the Additional Parameters and Plugins section.

3.  Enter the following:

postbumper.plugin=true&postbumper.width=0&postbumper.height=0&postbumper.lockUI=false&postbumper.playOnce=false&postbumper.path=bumperPlugin.swf&postbumper.relativeTo=video&postbumper.position=before&postbumper.includeInLayout=false&postbumper.preSequence=false 

4.  Click Go.

Whenever both pre-rolls and bumper videos are enabled, pre-rolls will always play before the bumper video. This is because the pre-roll is configured as, preSequence=1, and the bumper video is, preSequence=2. You can change the play order via the uiconf.  Post-roll ads will always play AFTER the bumper post-rolls.
