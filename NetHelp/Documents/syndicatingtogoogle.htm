Syndicating to Google

You can submit your videos to the Google search engine which will ultimately direct more traffic from Google to your website. Submission of video feeds to Google is free of charge, yet requires some technical knowledge. It is recommended that your website's webmaster be available for assistance. Google syndication is used mainly for submitting your site map and being certain that your media is indexed by Google.

The workflow for setting up Google syndication is divided into the following tasks:

•      Create a Landing Page.

•      Google Webmaster Tasks

•      VMC Publisher Tasks
