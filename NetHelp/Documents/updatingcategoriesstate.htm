Updating Categories’ State

Some of the category related actions taken in the VMC may trigger a category update process on the Vidiun backend.  This is mainly to allow continuous work through the VMC interface even when actions affect large portions of the category tree or apply on a very high number of categories.

While the Vidiun backend process is running, an ‘Updating Categories’ indication is set on the top right corner of the categories filter bar.

 

The ‘Updating Categories’ state may last a few minutes and until its completion. Some category settings and tree displays in the VMC may not be fully updated.

After the updating process is completed the ‘Updating Categories’ indication automatically disappears.
